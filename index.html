The problem

You finish the day not really sure where 8 hours went.

Chrome's built-in History tells you the URL list. It doesn't answer the questions you actually have.

"How long did I work today?"

Counting URLs in History is meaningless — you can't tell 30s glances from 30-min reading sessions. So you guess, and the guess feels off.

"Why am I tired but feel like I did nothing?"

Attention-switching costs are invisible. Without focus-block data, you'll never see that 14 context switches an hour are eating you alive.

"Which 'important' sites do I actually use?"

That bookmark you starred 6 months ago? You haven't opened it. The site you swore you'd "look at later"? Same. Without numbers, the story you tell yourself doesn't match reality.

Frequently asked

Things people ask before installing.

Will my browsing data ever leave my device? +

No. SiteLogs has no server. Visit data lives in your browser's IndexedDB on this device only. The only outbound calls are Google Fonts CSS (will self-host) and our license-key activation flow when you pay. See the full Privacy Policy.

Is this a subscription? +

No. $15 once, yours forever. Includes every current and future feature. The first 100 customers get early-bird $10.

Can I use one license on multiple devices? +

Yes — up to 5 devices on a single license key. Free up a slot from Settings → License → Deactivate this device.

Can I export or wipe my data? +

Yes — Settings → Data → Export JSON dumps everything; Wipe all data clears IndexedDB and chrome.storage. Uninstalling the extension also removes everything stored in your browser.

What if I don't like it? +

30-day no-questions-asked refund. Email us with your order ID and we'll process it the same day. Refund policy.

Does my visit data sync across devices? +

No — and on purpose. Your settings (rules, tags, pinned sites, budgets) sync via Chrome Sync if you have it on. Visit records stay on each device, so the maintainer never has access to them.
