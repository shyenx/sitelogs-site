Common requests

Refund (within 30 days)

Email us with your order ID from the purchase confirmation email. We'll process it the same day, no questions asked.

Lost license key

The original purchase confirmation email contains it. Search for "SiteLogs license". If you can't find it, email us with the address you used to buy.

Activation slot exhausted

Open Settings → License on a device you no longer use, click "Deactivate this device". The slot is freed instantly. If you've lost access to that device, email us.

Feature suggestion

Just email us with the idea and a sentence about how it would fit your workflow. We read every one.
