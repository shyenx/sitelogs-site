A note from the maintainer

Why we built it.

A memory tool disguised as a time tracker.

Each browsing action you take might feel insignificant. Every minute spent gets no clear value feedback. But these moments shouldn't be forgotten.

A week, a month, a year from now, when you look back — you'll see the heatmap glowing at 3 a.m., and remember the night that kept you awake. You'll see the long block on a domain you'd long forgotten you cared about, and remember the project that almost was. You'll see the gap on a Saturday and remember the day nobody else marked on a calendar.

Every record is an anchor we drop in the river of time. Lamps along the path you walked.

One day, when you turn around to look, it won't be confusion or darkness you see. You'll see how far you've come.

不被记忆，是永远的死亡。
To be unremembered is the only true death.

Most "productivity" software treats your time as inventory to be optimized. SiteLogs treats your time as a story to be remembered.

Same data. Different soul.
